Harvest 2022

September 27 2022

It is not the abundance, but the excellence of our grapes,
which will forge the reputation of the 2022 vintage! (*)

The harvest is coming to an end and it has kept all its promises.
Started for the earliest crus from August 20,
it lasted more than four long weeks under a blazing sun.

This is the seventh harvest of this 21st century, which started in August, which says a lot
on an irremediable change in the climate of our northern regions.
Despite meteorological parameters bringing their share of heat waves
during the vegetative period, some rains miraculously fell at the right time
enabling grapes of each variety and of all territorial origins
to complete their maturation in excellent conditions.

Each winegrower took the time to choose their harvest date with precision,
appreciating the tasting of the berries as much as the technical analyses.
The presses gave off aromatic expressions worthy of a Garden of Eden
and the parameters of the musts express a very high quality.
We achieved very good yields and allowed our winegrowers
to produce a material of exceptional quality
after a severe agronomic setback in 2021.
Balanced acidity and an average alcohol potential of 10.5°
will allow our winemakers to express all their talent
to convert these potentials into clear wines, then blended into Champagne wine.

It is still too early to qualify the style of this year, so providential.
It already holds all the characteristics of a solar vintage
whose reputation is second to none.

It has been a long time since Champagne knew
conditions that are just as favorable to the prestige and quality of our wines.
Be sure to find new emotions when tasting them in a few years.

Freely inspired by a thought by Joseph Joubert (1754 – 1824)

Commandeur Christophe JUAREZ
